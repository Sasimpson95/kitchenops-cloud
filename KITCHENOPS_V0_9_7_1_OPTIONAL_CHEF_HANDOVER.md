# KitchenOps v0.9.7.1 – Optional Chef Handover

- Chefs now have a Handover navigation link so they can choose to open it.
- Chef dashboard includes a small optional Today's Handover card.
- No acknowledgement, mark-as-read, or blocking step is required.
- Chef users remain read-only on Handover.
- Managers and Operations continue to write Tomorrow's Handover.
